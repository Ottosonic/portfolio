Critical Role

A look into the second season of a role-playing game, live-streamed by some nerdy voice actors.

What is Critical Role?

Voice actor Matthew Mercer leads a group of fellow voice actors on epic Dungeons & Dragons campaigns. These familiar voices bring the audience into the full experience of D&D, allowing imaginations to soar as the characters embark on adventures. This is Critical Role!

The cast.

The cast of Critical Role includes Matthew Mercer, Taliesin Jaffe, Liam O'Brien, Marisha Ray, Laura Bailey, Travis Willingham, Sam Riegel, and Ashley Johnson Ashley Johnson.
