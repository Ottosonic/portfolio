The Titans

The modern internet is vastly different from it’s early iterations in 1989. It’s come a long way from a black and green. We’ve gotten to this point thanks to many different developers. They’ve paved the way towards a universally accessible and usable web. These people are the titans of the web. Each of their skills have lead to a better web, pushing the limits and discovering new ways to make wonderful sites.
