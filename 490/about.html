What is this page about?

In short: this page exists to show you the wonderful people who have helped shape the web into what it is today. The reason why this site exists is that it stands as a testament to what I’ve learned over the last four years in University while studying front-end design. I wanted a way to express the joy I have for front-end work. This site represents all that I love about digital design.

“Creativity is nothing but a mind set free.”

-Torrie T. Asai

This project was made for a capstone class for my undergraduate degree. There is an essay and poster made in culmination for this project. You can thank GIT for all the skills and talent thrown into this project! Give the paper and poster a look here, and check out ASU’s GIT program.
