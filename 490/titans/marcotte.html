ethan marcotte

Ethan Marcotte has had a large impact on the modern Web. He is responsible for “responsive web design,” allowing websites to function on mobile devices.

He presents and speaks often at conferences and is active in the web design community.

"I’ve helped organizations design and build better sites and services, and to ensure they work everywhere. And in my work I’ve been fortunate enough to help clients like People Magazine, The Sundance Film Festival, and The Boston Globe to refine their design processes, improve their design systems, and to build more accessible products." ethanmarcotte.com
